Gene Expression Remix

Tune a cell so it produces the right amount of protein at the right time—without wasting energy.

Your mission

Cells constantly adjust which genes are used and how much protein is produced. In this simulation, you will control four levels of gene expression during a real-life situation.

Keep it personal, not private. Choose a routine life situation. You never need to share a diagnosis, medication, or private health information.
Purpose
Use cause-and-effect reasoning to explain how chromatin, transcription factors, mRNA stability, and translation work together to regulate protein production.
What you will do
  • Meet a cell's immediate protein need.
  • Adapt when one regulatory process is disrupted.
  • Scale production back when the need passes.
  • Complete brief explanations and download one PDF for Canvas.
Criteria for success
Your settings must meet each protein target within the energy budget. Your explanations should use evidence from your own simulation and accurately connect a control to its effect on protein production.
