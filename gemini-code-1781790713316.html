🎥 3. How to Record & Embed via Kaltura

Do not upload raw `.mp4` or `.mov` files directly to the discussion dashboard—they overload Canvas. Instead, use the integrated institutional streaming server, Kaltura ("My Media").

💡 Performance Tip: Resize this window side-by-side with your outline text from Step 2 to practice your delivery without script-reading!
  1. Look at the far left global navigation sidebar in Canvas and click on your Account icon, then select My Media.
  2. Near the top right corner of your video library, click the blue + Add New button and choose Express Capture (or Record in Browser).
  3. Grant your web browser microphone and camera access permissions, click the red button, deliver your structured briefing, and click Stop Recording.
  4. Select Use This to upload it, rename your file (e.g., Bio101_TumorBoard_CaseA), and hit Save.
  5. Return to this Discussion page, click the Reply textbox to bring up the text editor toolbar, and click the Embed Kaltura Media icon (it looks like a multi-colored starburst/wheel spoke).
  6. Find your recorded briefing in the list and click the Embed button next to it.
